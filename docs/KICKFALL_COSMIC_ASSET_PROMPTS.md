# Cosmic Foundry generated-asset briefs

Mode: built-in image generation. Images 1 and 2 supplied by the user were used
as style references only. Runtime images were copied into the project,
downscaled, and—where noted—chroma-keyed locally.

## Base backdrop

Create a full-bleed 16:9 layered game-background base: a quiet deep-navy
outer-space sky above a distant futuristic city, with sparse subtle stars and
city mass concentrated at the bottom and extreme sides. Preserve low contrast
through all gameplay corridors. Use premium polished 2D mobile-arcade rendering
in midnight blue with restrained cyan/violet windows. Exclude the Moon, Milky
Way, clouds, platforms, gates, balls, characters, HUD, frame, text, logos, and
watermarks.

## Milky Way layer

Create one broad violet/indigo/cool-blue Milky Way band sweeping diagonally from
lower-left to upper-right on perfectly uniform black. Keep it softly feathered,
irregular, restrained, and suitable for additive blending. Exclude all other
scene objects, text, logos, and watermarks.

## Moon layer

Create one complete blue-white full Moon centered on perfectly uniform black,
with recognizable lunar texture, a cool cyan rim, restrained halo, generous
padding, and no cropped edges. Exclude stars, city, nebula, platforms, UI, text,
logos, and watermarks.

## Lane material

Create an orthographic, repeatable horizontal platform fascia made from dark
navy gunmetal beveled panels, a continuous cyan-white top edge, restrained cyan
lower insets, and occasional amber service lights. Keep top and bottom parallel,
avoid perspective/end caps, and make the left/right edges repeatable. Exclude
all scene objects, traps, arrows, text, logos, and watermarks.

## Energy gate

Create one front-facing tall armored navy gate on uniform `#ff00ff`, with cyan
edge accents and exactly three readable amber energy cells connected by
symmetrical circuit seams. Keep a strong compact 1:1.8 silhouette and no cast
shadow or external halo. Exclude all other objects, text, logos, and watermarks.

## Catch rail

Create one low wide navy magnetic brake channel on uniform `#ff00ff`, with two
cyan roller nodes and an empty recessed center ready to receive a runtime ball.
The first generated variant included a ball; the final edit removed only that
ball and reconstructed the empty channel while preserving the hardware. Exclude
platforms, arrows, labels, text, logos, shadows, and watermarks.

## Kick cleat

Create one low navy base plate on uniform `#ff00ff`, holding exactly three short
rounded amber energy teeth in a row with tiny cyan status slits. Keep queued
balls readable and exclude balls, platforms, arrows, labels, text, logos,
shadows, and watermarks.
